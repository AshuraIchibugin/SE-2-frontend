Novur - Shop PSD

How To Edit PSD Files

In order to edit the file, you need to have Adobe Photoshop installed on your system, and the fonts mentioned in the credits installed.
As you can see on the in the image below, the layers are well organized and gropued into folders, which make editing very easy.
So, all you have to do, is select the layer, and then edit it with Adobe Photoshop, also, you can change Layer style, by double-clicking on it.
Enjoy the journey.
